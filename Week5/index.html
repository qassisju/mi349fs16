Extreme windstorms in many parts of the country pose a serious threat to buildings and their occupants. Your residence may be build "to code" but that does not mean it can withstand winds from extreme events such as tornadoes and major hurricanes. The purpose of a safe room or a wind shelter is to provide a space where you and your family can seek refuge that provides a high level of protection. You can build a safe room in one of several places in your home
